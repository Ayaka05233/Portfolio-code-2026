Design Process Book

Editorial

This is a book I created during my first year at OCAD. We created a short process book using materials from previous projects, such as notes and scrap paper. This project taught me to be experimental and less rigid with my work. While grids and guidelines can help create an organized and consistent look, they can sometimes narrow the way we think. I believe there’s no single way of approaching things, and this project helped me understand that.
