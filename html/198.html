198 Magazine Logo Animation

2D Motion

The logo was animated for the website of 198 Magazine, a Toronto-based photography publication. It was the first time I animated a walk cycle using traditional frame-by-frame techniques. This project made me want to become a motion designer, as it helped me understand the possibilities of motion design and how it can help elevate a project.
