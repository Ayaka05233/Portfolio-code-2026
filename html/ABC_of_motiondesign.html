ABC of Motion Design

2D Motion Design

This project combines the different motion design techniques I’ve learned in school. Until working on this project, I never truly paid attention to or tried out most of these techniques myself. Actually applying these methods to my project helped me understand that cool simulations or effects are not the only things that make a motion great. It’s the small and subtle techniques that make a project truly stand out.
