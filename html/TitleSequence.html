Nexus Title Sequence

Motion Design

This is a title sequence for a movie about a hivemind virus that infects people through water. Everything in this title sequence, including the 3D particles, was created using After Effects. My initial plan was to create the abstract shapes in Cinema 4D. However, due to technical difficulties and device limitations during this time, I ended up using After Effects. Working with limitations forced me to create the “illusion” of an underwater environment, which ultimately helped deepen my understanding of compositing and its possibilities.
